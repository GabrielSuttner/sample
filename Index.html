Conveniently Eat On Campus


Rated at 4.55 stars in ease of use, it’s no wonder that Dining Dollars holders enjoy being able to use their meal plan all over campus. Try Legend’s savory salmon, or Harvey’s portobello fontina panini. Salivating yet? It only begins here with Dining Dollars

Affordable


Think about how much you typically purchase food in a month? It may be more than what you think. Dining Dollars is only $125 a month for a semester. If you spend that much on food normally, you might as well save while you are at it.

Discounted Great Filling Meals

Everything you buy with Dining Dollars is tax free. On top on that, Dining Dollars also provides great discount at the all-you-care-to-eat diner, the Cannon Commons. Stop by to enjoy many dishes, including a flavor-packed Navajo Taco, for an average of 25% off.
